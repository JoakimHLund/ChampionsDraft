Champions League Draft

How it works

  • Pick 5 teams from each tournament — Champions League, Europa League, and Conference League. Total: 15 teams.
  • Earn points from match results + progression bonuses.
  • Each team’s score is multiplied by its Pot.
  • Highest total wins.
