Champions League Betting Game

Rules:

  • Each team is assigned to a Pot based on their ranking (1 to 4).
  • You can select up to 5 teams for your draft.
  • Scoring System:
    • Match Win: 3 points
    • Match Draw: 1 point
    • Progression Bonuses:
      • Qualify Directly to Round of 16 (Top 8): 5 bonus points
      • Qualify via Playoffs: 3 bonus points
      • Advancing to Quarter-finals: 4 bonus points
      • Advancing to Semi-finals: 6 bonus points
      • Advancing to Final: 8 bonus points
      • Winning the Final: 10 bonus points
  • Multipliers are applied based on team pots:
    • Pot 1: x1 multiplier
    • Pot 2: x1.5 multiplier
    • Pot 3: x2 multiplier
    • Pot 4: x2.5 multiplier
